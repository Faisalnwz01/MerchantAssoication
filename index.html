Welcome to Pakistan American Merchant Association(PAMA)

Since its founding, PAMA has responded to changing community needs and problems. When the agency began, the presence of Pakistani merchants was nonexistent. PAMA was designed to help Pakistani businesses increase workflow and help the community get acquitted with existing businesses. From helping Merchants with legal advice to organizing Mela, PAMA has always been there for our community. As community needs changing, PAMA responded with new programs. Today our Annual Mela is the biggest gathering of Pakistani-American in NYC.

Brooklyn Mela

Brooklyn Mela is an annual event held on Coney Island Avenue since 1995. Mela is held on Sunday of Pakistani Independence day. It is a joyous occasion, where thousands of New Yorkers meet up to celebrate Pakistani Independence day.

Music, food, and many a reveler will fill Coney Island Avenue, between Avenue H and Newkirk Avenue, this Sunday for the annual Brooklyn Mela, which celebrates Pakistan’s independence day. Sponsored by the Pakistani-American Merchants Association, the festival will be held from 12pm to 6pm.
